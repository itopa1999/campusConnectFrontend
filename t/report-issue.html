Harassment, threats, impersonation, fraud attempt, spam, unsafe meetup behavior, or policy violation.

Technical bug

Login issues, broken pages, listing upload problems, missing dashboard data, or errors while using the site.

Question or feature

Ask how something works, suggest a feature, request improvement, or share product feedback.

Account or verification

Email verification, student status, profile corrections, password access, or account safety questions.

Other support

Anything else that does not fit the categories above. We will route it to the right place.

What to include

  • Your Demo University email and the email or profile name of the other user, if relevant.
  • The listing title, listing URL, screenshot, transaction amount, and date of the incident.
  • A clear timeline of what happened and what outcome you are requesting.

Safety first

  • If there is an immediate threat, contact campus security or local emergency support first.
  • Do not send more money while a report is being reviewed.
  • Keep conversation records and avoid deleting evidence until the case is resolved.
