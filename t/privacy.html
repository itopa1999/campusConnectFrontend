CampusConnect UI

Privacy Policy

📅 Last updated: May 2026 | Version 1.0

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how CampusConnect UI collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information when you use our platform – a marketplace exclusively for verified University of Ibadan students.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information: When you register, we collect your full name, UI email address (@ui.edu.ng or @stu.ui.edu.ng), phone number, hall of residence, department, and a password (hashed).
1.2 Profile Data: You may optionally add a profile picture, and we store your listings (photos, descriptions, prices, meetings preferences).
1.3 Usage Data: We automatically collect information such as IP address, device type, browser, pages visited, time spent, and interactions (messages, reports, clicks).
1.4 Verification Data: If you request hall verification, we may collect your room number and a confirmation from the hall porter.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 To create and manage your account, and to verify your status as a current UI student.
2.2 To enable the core marketplace features – posting listings, searching, messaging, and completing transactions.
2.3 To improve safety: detect fraud, enforce our Terms & Conditions, and respond to reports.
2.4 To communicate with you about your listings, messages, platform updates, or security notices.
2.5 To analyze usage patterns and improve the platform’s performance and user experience.
2.6 To calculate and collect transaction fees (if applicable) and provide payment-related features.

3. Information Sharing & Disclosure

3.1 Within the Platform: Other users can see your name, hall of residence, listing activity, and ratings. Your email address and phone number are not publicly displayed but may be shared when you initiate a chat or transaction.
3.2 Service Providers: We may share data with third‑party services that help us operate (e.g., hosting, analytics, email delivery). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
3.3 Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of CampusConnect UI, its users, or the public.
3.4 University Cooperation: In serious cases of fraud, harassment, or illegal activity, we may share relevant information with the University of Ibadan authorities.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures (encryption, secure servers, access controls) to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your password confidential and for logging out after each session.

5. Your Rights & Choices

5.1 Access & Correction: You can review and update most of your account information via profile settings.
5.2 Deletion: You may delete your account at any time. This will remove your profile and listings, but certain transaction records may be retained for legal reasons.
5.3 Opt‑out: You can unsubscribe from non‑essential emails (e.g., newsletters) using the link in the email. Transactional and security emails cannot be opted out of.
5.4 Cookies: We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and to remember preferences. Your browser settings allow you to block cookies, but some features may not work properly.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. After account deletion, we may retain anonymized data for analytics and to comply with legal obligations (e.g., fraud records for up to 2 years).

7. Third‑Party Links

Our platform may contain links to external sites (e.g., WhatsApp, payment gateways). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

8. Children’s Privacy

CampusConnect UI is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via email or a prominent notice on the platform. The “Last updated” date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised. Your continued use of CampusConnect UI after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
