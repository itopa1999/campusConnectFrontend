About me

From a student's need to a full‑fledged platform

As a student enterprenuer, I saw how difficult it was to safely buy and sell used items on campus. External marketplaces are full of strangers, delivery fees, and scams. I wanted a solution that was exclusively for students, with built‑in trust and zero fees.

That's how CampusConnect was born. A growing community of verified students. Today, it's helping students save money and connect with each other.
