About me

From a student's need to a full‑fledged platform

As a UI student, I saw how difficult it was to safely buy and sell used items on campus. External marketplaces are full of strangers, delivery fees, and scams. I wanted a solution that was exclusively for students, with built‑in trust and zero fees.

That's how CampusConnect was born – a weekend project that grew into a community of over 500 verified students. Today, it's helping students save money and connect with each other.

“I built CampusConnect because I believe that students can solve their own problems – given the right tools and a trusted community.”
