How CampusConnect works

Designed for UI students, by a UI student. Zero complexity, maximum safety.

1. Sign up with UI email

Use your @ui.edu.ng or @stu.ui.edu.ng — only current students get access. Instant verification.

2. Post what you want to sell

Add photos, set a price, choose category (textbooks, electronics, furniture, etc.). Takes 1 minute.

3. Meet on campus & exchange

Chat, agree on a spot (library, faculty lobby, common room). Cash or transfer — no delivery stress.
