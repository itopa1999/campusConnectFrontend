How CampusConnect works

Designed for UI students, by a UI student. Zero complexity, maximum safety.

1. Sign up with UI email

Use your @ui.edu.ng or @stu.ui.edu.ng email. Only current students get access.

Starter credits: New accounts receive 3 free points to post items. Each posting uses 1 point.
2. Post what you want to sell

Add photos, set a price, choose category (textbooks, electronics, furniture, etc.). Takes 1 minute.

Posting requirement: To create a listing you must have at least 1 point in your account. New accounts receive 3 free points on signup. Each listing costs 1 point. Visit your profile to check or top up your balance.
3. Meet at a safe campus spot

Chat, choose a public UI meetup point, inspect the item, then complete payment directly with the buyer or seller by cash or bank transfer. CampusConnect only connects you.
